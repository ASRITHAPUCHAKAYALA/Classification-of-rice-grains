Types of Rice

Basmati

Long, slender-grained aromatic rice traditionally from India and Pakistan.

Jasmine

Fragrant rice variety from Thailand, with a soft and slightly sticky texture.

Arborio

Italian short-grain rice used to make creamy risottos.

Karacadag

A variety native to Turkey, often used in traditional dishes.

Ipsala

Grown in Turkey's Ipsala region, known for its quality and fluffiness.
